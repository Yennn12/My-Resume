ACHIEVEMENT

An achievement is a tangible and intangible accomplishment or success that is reached through effort, skill, perseverance, or a combination of these factors. Achievements can be personal, professional, academic, or related to various aspects of life.

EDUCATION BACKGROUND

Education background refers to a person's history and experiences in the field of education with academic qualifications, institutions attended, and degrees earned.

CO-CURRICULUM ACTIVITIES

Co-curricular activities, often referred to as extracurricular activities, are activities or experiences that complement the formal academic curriculum in an educational institution.

VOLUNTARY ACTIVITIES

Volutary activities refer to tasks, projects, or services that individuals undertake willingly and without monetary compensation. In voluntary activities, individuals offer their time, skills, and efforts to contribute to a cause or organization that aligns with their interests, values, or a desire to make a positive impact on society.

SKILLS

Skill a person's ability to perform specific tasks or activities effectively, often acquired through training, experience, or education.
